Privacy Policy

MyRate: Currency Exchange

Last updated: February 25, 2026

1. Introduction

LP Wave ("we," "our," or "us") operates MyRate: Currency Exchange. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected when you use our application.

By using MyRate, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Preferences: Your favorite currency pairs and default currencies
  • Rate Alerts: Target exchange rates and notification preferences you configure

Automatically collected:

  • Device Information: Device type and operating system version
  • Usage Data: App interactions and feature usage (anonymized)
  • Location (optional): Country-level location to suggest a default currency, only if you grant permission

MyRate does not require you to create an account. No email or password is collected.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the MyRate service
  • To deliver rate alert notifications you have set
  • To remember your preferred currencies across sessions
  • To improve and optimize the app experience
  • To respond to your support requests

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

We may share data only with:

  • Exchange Rate Providers: We fetch live rate data from third-party APIs. These services do not receive your personal information.
  • Push Notification Service: To deliver rate alerts to your device (e.g., Apple APNs / Google FCM)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by applicable law

5. Data Security

All data is stored locally on your device or transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS/TLS) connections. We do not maintain a central user database for MyRate. Your preferences stay on your device.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: All your preferences are visible and editable directly in the app
  • Delete: Clear all app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage in your device settings
  • Opt out of notifications: Disable alerts at any time in the app or in your device notification settings
  • Revoke location permission: Deny or revoke location access at any time in your device settings

7. Children's Privacy

MyRate is a general-purpose utility app suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since no account registration is required, no personal data is collected during normal use.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last updated" date above.

Privacy Policy

Zumi: Family Manager

Last updated: November 27, 2025

1. Introduction

LP Wave ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Zumi: Family Manager ("App").

By accessing or using our App, you signify that you have read, understood, and agree to our collection, storage, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Service.

2. Information We Collect

To provide the features of Zumi (finance tracking, task management, contact syncing), we may collect the following types of information:

A. Personal Information You Provide:

  • Account Data: Name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Family Profile Data: Names or nicknames of family members (including children) added to the family group.
  • Financial Data: Information about budget, expenses, allowances, and savings goals that you manually input. Note: We do not store credit card numbers or bank login credentials directly.
  • Task Data: Details of chores and tasks you create.

B. Information We Access on Your Device (Permissions):

  • Contacts (READ_CONTACTS): We request access to your contact list only if you choose to share specific contacts (e.g., emergency numbers, doctors) with your family group. We do not upload your entire address book to our servers for marketing purposes.
  • Notifications: To send reminders about tasks and bill payments.

C. Automatically Collected Information:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the App (e.g., features used, time spent).
  • Device Data: IP address, device model, operating system version (used for analytics and troubleshooting).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionality (e.g., syncing data between family members' devices).
  • To manage your account and family group.
  • To generate financial reports and summaries for your personal use.
  • To improve the App and fix bugs.
  • To communicate with you regarding updates or customer support.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

We may share data with trusted third-party service providers solely to operate the App:

  • Cloud Storage & Backend: We use secure cloud services (e.g., Google Firebase, AWS) to store your data so it syncs across devices.
  • Analytics: We may use services like Google Analytics for Firebase to understand App usage trends (anonymized data).

5. Children's Privacy

Zumi is designed for families. However, we do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under the age of 13 without parental consent.

  • The App is intended to be managed by a parent or guardian ("Family Manager").
  • Parents create profiles for their children within the App.
  • If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child without parental consent, please contact us, and we will delete it.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures (such as encryption) to protect your personal and financial information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your account and all associated data. You can do this directly within the App settings or by contacting us.
  • Correction: Update incorrect information via the App settings.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
